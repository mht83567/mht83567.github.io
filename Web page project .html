OKC Thunder Playoff Season 2016

This was the 8th season for the Oklahoma City Thunder and it was a good one. With a new head coach, Billy Donovan, the Thunder were pretty successful. In the 2016 playoff season the Oklahoma City Thunder won the Northwestern Division and clinched the third seed in the Western Conference. They also made it to the Western Conference Finals, it was the 4th time to go the conference finals in 6 season. They fought a tough battle from the beginning to the end. Playing and defeating the Dallas Mavericks and then the San Antonio Spurs, but unfortunately they had a chance to make it to the finals, but couldn't defeat the Golden State Warriors.
